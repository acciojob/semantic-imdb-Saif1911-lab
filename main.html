Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. He is offered a chance to have his past crimes erased if he can perform the impossible task of planting an idea into someone's mind — an act known as inception.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Elliot Page, Tom Hardy, and Ken Watanabe. Each actor delivers a powerful performance that adds depth to the complex narrative.
